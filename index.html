The lake

Where everything is

Lac d'Annecy is a bent finger pointing south: the wide Grand Lac in the north, the narrower Petit Lac curving around Duingt's pinch point. Our house is the green star.

Savoyard greatest hits

Tartiflette
Potatoes, lardons, onions, half a wheel of Reblochon melted on top. Group dinner.
Fondue savoyarde
Comté + Beaufort + Emmental, white wine, garlic, bread, problem solved.
Raclette
Melt, scrape, repeat. Charcuterie + cornichons + boiled potatoes. Easy.
Diots
Smoky Savoyard sausages, often slow-cooked in white wine. Mountain energy.
Féra du lac
Local lake whitefish. Almost only eaten right here. Order it lake-edge.
Boulangerie run
Croissants, pain au chocolat, baguette tradition. Whoever's up first. Non-negotiable.
Evenings & what's on

Stuff actually happening Aug 15–29

Aug 18 – 28 · on our dates
Impérial Annecy Festival

Jazz, classical, comedy on the lake. Free 6pm discovery concerts, paid 9pm headliners in the Imperial Gardens. Easy pre-dinner move.

Highlights: People Aki (Aug 23, 6pm, free) · Edouard Baer (Aug 28, 9pm, paid).

Aug 19 – 20 · on our dates
Château de Montrottier · knights & equestrian show

Pair with Gorges du Fier (Lovagny) for a half-day combo. Easy bring-everyone day.

All trip · default mode
Annecy Paysages outdoor art walk

Free citywide outdoor art trail. Wander, stumble, gelato.

Aug 1 · misses our dates
Fête du Lac (legendary, but we're 2 weeks late)

The biggest fireworks show in Europe over the lake. Annecy's signature event — but it happens before we land. Filing as: another reason to come back.

Default evening: sunset swim → long dinner on the terrace or at a lake-edge place → maybe a 9pm Impérial show, maybe an espresso in old town, maybe just bed. We're not here for nightlife — we're here for the long table.
